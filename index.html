Bridging Educational Gaps

We are a group of highly motivated university students and recent graduates who seeks to empower students in JHS and SHS in underprevilleged communities to achieve their full potentials.

Find Out More

This is what we do!

EduGap is in two phases. The first is more of empowerment, education and mentorship. This is basically reaching out to the students in underprivileged communities with students who have been able to thrive under similar conditions. These students will serve as mentors as well as educate them on possibilities in education and how they should set their career goals and work towards it. This will be an attempt to break their inferiority complex and let them know that with hard work everything is attainable.

The second phase of the solution will be more practical and geared towards teaching students in high school the actual skills we believe is lacking. This would be training centers where students are introduced to the variety of new Technologies and software like MS word, Excel, Google, Youtube and others. We would also introduce those that will be interested to entrepreneurship and Computer programming. These are attempts to ensure that students who graduate from High school are tach versed and are ready for University and life in general.

Main Highlights

Empowerment

We empower students from rural areas to break away any inferiority complex in them and let them know their goals and dreams are possible

Education

We introduce students to some of the current technologies that are making waves and how they can use them to their advantages.

Entrepreneurship

We introduce students to entrepreneurship, we believe they can solve some pecuiliar problems in their community if their minds are drawn to possible ways they kind identify problems and clearly define it to solve it.

Preparation for Next level

We prepare JHS students to make them ready for SHS working with them through their career aspirations and courses they may need to study and how they can plan and make the most out of SHS. Also SHS students are worked with to make them ready for tertiary institutions
